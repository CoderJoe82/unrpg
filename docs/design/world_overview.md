# World Overview

## Theme: Eco-Integration
A fantasy world where nature and civilization exist in harmony

## Key Concepts
- Balance between natural and civilized spaces
- Magic enhances rather than replaces nature
- Environmental respect is fundamental

## Major Areas
1. Arbor-City: Tree-integrated city
2. Mountain-Home: Dwarven caves
3. River-Port: Coastal trading hub

## Factions
- Mage's Guild: Magical research
- Druid Circle: Nature guardians
- Forgeborn: Master crafters
- Spy Covenant: Information brokers

## Races
- Elves: Nature-attuned, magical
- Dwarves: Crafters, resilient
- Humans: Adaptable, varied
- Vargr: Wolf-folk, tribal
